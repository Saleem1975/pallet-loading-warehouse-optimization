# Business Impact

## Why this matters

Pallet loading affects warehouse efficiency, transportation cost, product damage, and customer satisfaction.

## Operational benefits

This approach can help organizations:

- Improve pallet volume utilization
- Reduce empty space on pallets
- Reduce product damage risk
- Improve stacking safety
- Make storage policies more realistic
- Account for humidity and storage time
- Improve logistics planning and distribution center performance

## Consulting applications

This work can be translated into consulting engagements involving:

- Warehouse utilization improvement
- Packaging optimization
- Logistics network operations
- Distribution center redesign
- Storage policy design
- Palletization decision support
- Supply chain process improvement
